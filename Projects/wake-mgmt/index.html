Wake Management

Wind farm wake modeling and optimization across wind conditions and yaw angles.

Overview

At GE Research, I built Gaussian process based models to learn the effect of wakes in a wind farm across different wind conditions and yaw angles, including measurement uncertainties. I developed an optimization framework to optimize wind turbine operation for wakes, improving overall farm efficiency.

Key Contributions

  • Built Gaussian process models to learn wake effects across wind conditions and yaw angles
  • Incorporated measurement uncertainties into the wake modeling framework
  • Developed optimization framework for wind turbine operation under wake conditions
  • Applied polynomial chaos and uncertainty quantification methods for robust optimization

Impact

Enabled more efficient wind farm operation by accurately modeling wake interactions and optimizing turbine yaw/operation to minimize energy loss across the farm.

Skills & Tools

Uncertainty quantification, reinforcement learning, Gaussian processes, polynomial chaos, optimization methods.
